Common Questions About Our Plans

Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and your billing adjusts accordingly.

What happens when I reach my daily task limit?

You'll receive a notification and the option to upgrade. Your daily limit resets at midnight UTC. Upgrading to Pro gives you 100 tasks/day.

Is there a free trial for Pro features?

The Free plan lets you explore core automation features. When you're ready for advanced capabilities like image-to-automation, upgrading is instant and hassle-free.

How does the Enterprise plan differ from Pro?

Enterprise includes unlimited tasks, custom templates, API access for external integrations, 1-year history retention, and priority support with a dedicated account manager.
